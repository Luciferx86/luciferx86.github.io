1. Introduction

Miscridex (“we”, “our”, “the app”) is a helper app for the online game Miscrits. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data. By using Miscridex, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

We collect minimal personal information only if you choose to create an account:

We also collect anonymized diagnostic, performance, and usage information through Firebase Analytics, Firebase Crashlytics, and Firebase Performance Monitoring (services provided by Google LLC) to help us understand app performance and improve stability. This may include events such as app installs, updates, screen views, and in-app interactions.

These services may automatically collect information such as device type, operating system, app version, screen views, feature usage, and crash logs. This data does not include personally identifiable information such as your name, email, or precise location, and it is not used for advertising or tracking across apps.

You can learn more about how Google handles Firebase data by visiting the Firebase Privacy and Security page.
