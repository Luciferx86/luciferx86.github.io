Evolutions

1. Keeper

Keeper spends its time hiding in the wild, honing its skills by kicking around rocks like balls. It likes to test its mettle in friendly matches against Fubby

2. Dribble

Dribble, skilled in precision and agility, enjoys challenging others to friendly matches. Whatever rock this Miscrit chooses hides something it deeply covets inside

3. Defender

Defender evolves into a tactical role, using its toughened hide to shield allies, intercept obstacles, and create barriers, steadfastly safeguarding its friends

4. Fortifield

Fortifield can grow large enough to host dozens of tiny Miscrits on its back, where they can play to their heart's content. Many see this gentle giant as a symbol of camaraderie
