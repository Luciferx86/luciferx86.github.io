Evolutions

1. Lagoony

Lagoony was first discovered in deep sea fishing nets. It appeared caught, but closer inspection showed it had eaten all the fish and was playfully mangling the expensive nets

2. Kreecha

Kreecha has been known to hunt giant squid and other large sea creatures. With its massive claws, even powerful sharks have little chance against it

3. Depthar

Depthar is so heavy it often walks the sea floor until spotting prey. Then, with a quaking slap of its massive hands, it rockets at whatever unfortunate beast has caught its eye

4. Blackfin

Blackfin lives at such depths that it is almost never seen. The only light glimpsing it comes from the explosive energy balls it conjures by vibrating its fins at insane speed
