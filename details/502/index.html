Evolutions

1. Dark Shellbee

Dark Shellbee never emerged suddenly—it has always been there, lurking in the deep, watching. Instead of seeking battle, it moves with quiet, calculated intent

2. Dark Enopen

Dark Enopen is neither curious nor affectionate. It avoids others, retreating beneath its shell to keep its true nature hidden from sight

3. Dark Amari

Dark Amari is not feared by children—it is forgotten. It waits in silence, unseen, until the moment is right to remind the world of its presence

4. Dark Kalamos

Dark Kalamos has a fixed, unchanging number of tentacles. However, no one can seem to agree on what that number actually is
