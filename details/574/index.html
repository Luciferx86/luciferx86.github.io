Evolutions

1. Smolderfry

Smolderfry's rock-like shell prevents its own heat from seeping out into the moon's atmosphere. It constantly releases any excess heat through its powerful punches

2. Blasterbuzz

The low gravity of the moon keeps Blasterbuzz from being weighted down by its large claw which it uses to pummel craters into the zone's surface

3. Pyroclash

Pyroclash is highly territorial to the point it will go out of its way to engage anything it sees in a fight, even if unprovoked. Usually, a Beateorite

4. Clawsteroid

Clawsteroid can easily punch massive meteors to dust in a split second. Scholars believe, if it weren't held back by the low gravity of the moon, a single attack could have catastrophic results
